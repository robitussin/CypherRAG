Microsoft Corporation was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975.
Bill Gates later became the CEO of Microsoft Corporation.
Paul Allen focused on other ventures.
In 2010, Microsoft Corporation released the Kinect device.
The Kinect device became popular among Xbox gamers.
Around the same time, Bill Gates started dedicating more of his time to the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation is also known as the Gates Foundation.
The Gates Foundation partnered with the World Health Organization.
The World Health Organization is abbreviated as WHO.
The Gates Foundation partnered with WHO to combat malaria.
Later, WHO collaborated with the United Nations.
The United Nations is abbreviated as UN.
WHO collaborated with UN in Geneva to coordinate global health efforts.
Microsoft Corporation continued to dominate the software market with its Windows operating system.
Microsoft Corporation is also called MS in some reports.
