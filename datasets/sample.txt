Microsoft Corporation, often referred to simply as Microsoft, was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975. 
Gates later became the CEO of Microsoft, while Allen focused on other ventures. 
In 2010, Microsoft released the Kinect device, which became popular among Xbox gamers.
Around the same time, Bill Gates started dedicating more of his time to the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation. 
The foundation, also known as the Gates Foundation, partnered with the World Health Organization (WHO) to combat malaria.
Later, WHO collaborated with the United Nations (UN) in Geneva to coordinate global health efforts. 
Microsoft, also called MS in some reports, continued to dominate the software market with its Windows operating system.
